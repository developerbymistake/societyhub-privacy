1 Who We Are

SocietyHub ("we", "us", "our") is a community management platform that connects residents with their society's administration, security team, and service providers.

This Privacy Policy applies to the SocietyHub mobile application available on the Google Play Store. By using the app you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Service Provider Business Information

The app's service directory displays business contact information for service providers registered in your community (e.g., plumbers, electricians, housekeeping services). This information — provider name, business phone number, address, service rates, and availability timings — is entered by your community's admin on behalf of the provider. Residents can use this information to contact providers directly by phone.

Provider business contact data is not personal data of app users. It is business information displayed as a service directory within your community.

We do NOT collect: email address, contacts from your phone, call logs, SMS, photos or camera, microphone audio, browsing history, installed app list, health or fitness data, or biometric data of any kind.

2a OTP Sign-In & Account Deletion

How sign-in works

SocietyHub uses phone number + OTP (One-Time Password) for account verification. When you enter your phone number, a one-time code is sent to your phone via SMS. We use a third-party SMS delivery service (Fast2SMS) to send this code. Your phone number is shared with Fast2SMS solely for the purpose of delivering the OTP. Fast2SMS does not receive any other personal data and is bound by confidentiality obligations. We never store or read your SMS messages.

Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from inside the app at any time:

  1. Open the app → go to Profile.
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Once confirmed, your profile, flat details, SOS history, location records, and activity data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. You may also request deletion by email — see Section 9.

Feedback & Reviews — what other residents can see

When you submit a star rating or written review for a service provider, your name and flat number are displayed alongside your review to other residents within your community. Reviews are visible only inside your community — they are not public on the internet. Writing a review is entirely optional. You may contact us to request removal of a review you submitted.

3 Location Data — Important Details

Location is only accessed when you actively press the SOS button. The app never collects your location silently, in the background, or at any other time.

What happens step by step

  1. You tap the SOS button and confirm the emergency.
  2. The app requests location permission at that moment (if not already granted).
  3. If you allow, the app reads your GPS coordinates once and sends them to our server as part of the emergency alert.
  4. Your location is displayed to your society's admin and security team so they can reach you quickly.
  5. If you deny the permission, the SOS alert still sends — it just won't include a location.

What we do NOT do with location

  • We do not track your location continuously or in the background.
  • We do not share your location with advertisers, marketing services, or any third party for commercial purposes.
  • We do not sell location data.
  • We do not use location data for analytics or profiling.

Storage

Your location at the time of an SOS is stored as part of the emergency record on our servers. It is visible only to your community's admin and security team. It is retained as part of your emergency history and is deleted when you request account deletion.

5 How We Use Your Data

  • Account & Identity: Verify your phone via OTP and authenticate you on every login.
  • Community Features: Display your profile, flat details, and community notices.
  • Payments: Process and record maintenance fee transactions via Razorpay.
  • Push Notifications: Send you community notices, payment confirmations, and SOS status updates via Firebase Cloud Messaging.
  • SOS Emergency Response: Relay your alert (and optional location) to your society's admin and security team in real time.
  • Service Directory: Connect you with service providers registered in your community.
  • App Improvement: Aggregate, anonymised usage patterns to improve performance and fix issues.

We do not use your data for advertising, cross-app tracking, or to build marketing profiles.

8 Data Security & Storage

  • All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Authentication tokens are stored in the device's encrypted secure storage (not shared preferences).
  • Our servers are access-controlled; only authorised personnel can access production data.
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure — we never see or store your card or bank details.

9 Data Retention & Account Deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account exists or as needed to provide the service.

How to delete your account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the app:

  1. Open the app → go to Profile.
  2. Tap Delete Account at the bottom.
  3. Confirm deletion.

Alternatively, email us at developerbymistake@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and your registered phone number. We will process your request within 30 days.

After deletion: your profile, flat details, SOS history (including stored location), and payment records are permanently removed from our servers. Payment transaction records required for accounting compliance may be retained in anonymised form for a legally required period.
